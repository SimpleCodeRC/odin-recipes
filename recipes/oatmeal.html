description

here is a paragraph or two describing the recipe

ingredients

  • plant-based milk
  • water
  • instant oats
  • ripe banana
  • frozen berries
  • hemp hearts
  • almond butter

steps

  1. chop 1/2 ripe banana into small pieces and throw into the pot
  2. pour 1/2 cup of plant-based milk and 1/2 cup of instant oats into the pot (add splash of water too)
  3. add frozen berries close to the end, cook for approximately 5 minutes on medium-heat and stir constantly
  4. top with hemp hearts and almond butter
